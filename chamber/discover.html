Discover Modesto

Gallo Center for the Arts

The Gallo Center for the Arts is Modesto's fine art hub. Many events are held here, whether they be social gatherings or musical performances.

Graceada Park

Graceada Park is a great place for children and adults alike. With a massive jungle gym, spacious amphitheater, and well-fitted water park, there's always fun to be had.

James C. Enochs High School

Enochs High School is just one of many quality public schools here in Modesto. Enochs boasts a large campus, a myriad of extracurriculars, and quality instruction.

McHenry Mansion

Robert McHenry is one of Modesto's most historic figures. Now his house, preserved by Mr. Gallo himself and maintained after all these years, provides priceless views into the past.

Dry Creek

Dry Creek splits from the Tuolumne River in the heart of Modesto and runs straight out eastward. This minor, yet strong, creek provides great hiking trails and picturesque backyards.
