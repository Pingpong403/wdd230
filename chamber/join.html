Membership
Membership Level:

Each membership level has different perks:

Non-Profit (free)

  • Free entry for up to 10 people in our monthly seminars
  • A day of ad space on our home page every month
  • Unlimited access for one representative to our leadership trainings

Bronze ($50/mo.)

  • Free entry for up to 20 people in our monthly seminars
  • Two days of ad space on our home page every month
  • Unlimited access for two representatives to our leadership trainings
  • A free tablet for every 5 people in your organization

Silver ($150/mo.)

  • Free entry for up to 30 people in our monthly seminars
  • Five days of ad space on our home page every month
  • Unlimited access for three representatives to our leadership trainings
  • A free tablet for every 4 people in your organization
  • Low priority access to our venues for events

Gold ($300/mo.)

  • Free entry for your entire organization in our monthly seminars
  • A week of ad space on our home page every month
  • Unlimited access for five representatives to our leadership trainings
  • A free tablet for every person in your organization
  • High priority access to our venues for events
  • Special recognition on our website, at our seminars, and in the papers
